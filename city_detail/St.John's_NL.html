Summary

Elimination of parking minimums city-wide: The city of St. John's eliminated minimum off-street parking requirements for the following three commercial land uses (if the gross floor area is 200 m2 or less): Restaurant, Retail, and Service Shop. Elimination of parking minimums in select downtown areas: The city of St. John's eliminated minimum off-street parking requirements for residential developments on Water Street or Duckworth Street having 5 Dwelling Units or less. Additionally, for all new change of use applications from non-residential to another non-residential use in the downtown parking area (where the floor area remains constant), the applicant will be exempt from all the minimum and maximum parking requirements in parking ratio table Section 8.3. Implementation of parking maximums city-wide: The city of St. John's also has maximum off-street parking requirements for all land uses. Reduction of parking requirements in select downtown areas: On May 27th 2022, the city of St. John's reduced minimum and maximum parking requirements by 50 percent for new non-residential developments in the downtown parking area.
