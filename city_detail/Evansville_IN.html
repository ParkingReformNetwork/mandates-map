Summary

All uses permitted in the C-3 central business district are exempt from minimum parking requirements except for residential uses, nurseries, nursery schools, child care centers, hotels and motels, and special uses. In residential zoning districts within the Arts Overlay Zone, off-street parking is not required. Within the West Franklin Overlay Zone, parking requirements are reduced by 25% and may be further reduced with bike parking. Parking requirements in the Jacobsville Overlay Zone may be reduced with bike parking.
