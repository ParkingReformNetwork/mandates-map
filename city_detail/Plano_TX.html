Summary

The BG Downtown Business/Government District is exempt from minimum parking requirements. Maximum parking is set at the minimum number of required off-street parking spaces plus 10%, which may be exceeded if conditions are met. Up to 10% of required parking may be permanently set aside for stormwater quality or quantity improvements provided that the minimum required parking is 100 spaces or greater.
