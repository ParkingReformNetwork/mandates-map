Summary

For all non-residential uses in the C-2 Central Business District the number of required parking spaces shall be fifty percent (50%) of the number otherwise required. Cash in lieu of parking may be authorized by the City Council for remaining requirements in the C-2 Central Business District. In all districts, on street parking directly in front of a lot may count toward fulfilling the parking requirement of that lot. One parking space credit may be given for every partial on-street parking space in front of the lot that is over fifty-percent (50%) of the length of the on-street parking space. In addition, an exemption of one thousand (1,000) square feet may be applied toward the calculation of required off-street parking spaces if on-street parking is provided adjacent to the proposed use.
