Summary

In the business A-1 and A-2 zone, small nonresidential uses are exempt. In the Business D, D-1, D-2 and BD-3 Districts, dwellings and healthcare uses are exempt. All uses in the CGD Zone are exempt. Various reductions are available across the city, and parking maximums apply is some of the business districts. New Haven Parking Authority has signed on to support DesegregateCT's platform, which includes eliminating parking requirements citywide.
