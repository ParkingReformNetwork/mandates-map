Summary

Allowed uses in the CD Downtown Commercial District are exempt from providing off-street parking spaces. Within the PO Pedestrian-Oriented Overlay District, non-residential uses are exempt from providing off-street parking spaces. No off-street parking spaces are required for residential building projects of 10 units or less, for larger residential buildings, parking requirements are reduced. Within the Central-City Special Parking District, the minimum off-street parking requirement for multi-dwelling units is reduced. Within locally designated historic districts, no off-street parking or loading spaces are required for rehabilitation or reuse of existing structures. For new construction within locally designated historic districts, minimum off-street parking ratios are reduced by 50 percent. No use may provide more than 150 percent of the minimum off-street parking, except through approval of an Alternative Parking Plan. Within the Pedestrian-Oriented Overlay, parking maximums are set at 100% of the minimums. Surface parking lots are prohibited in the Downtown Core District and Warehouse District.
