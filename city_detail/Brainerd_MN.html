Summary

Previously, Brainerd had no off street parking requirements for the following zones: B-4 (General Business) District, B-5 (Commercial Amusement) District, B-6 (Washington Street Commercial) District, I-1 (Light Industry) District, I-2 (General Industry) District. On October 16, 2023, the city added parking minimums to the TC Town Center and MS Main Street districts at a reduced requirement. All other nonresidential uses outside of these districts are now exempt from parking requirements. Parking maximums apply to all nonresidential uses and bike parking may substitute for required automobile parking.
