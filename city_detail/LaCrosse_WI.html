Summary

On March 10, 2022 La Crosse eliminated parking mandates for non-residential uses in Local Business, Commercial, and Community Business zones. Previously La Crosse had no parking requirements in Industrial zones, as well as exemptions for the downtown and uptown areas. In early 2024, the city allowed ADUs without parking and a further reduction or full elimination of parking minimums is proposed.
