Summary

Since at least 2008, the city has implemented a parking exemption for every use in some sectors, except for residential units. The exemption is applied by zones and they are mostly within the old pre-war downtown area. Also, anywhere else in the city and for every uses, the parking requirement can be reduced by a maximum of 30% of the required number of spaces with a monetary compensation of 7500$ per missing spot. The city council decides if the reduction is accepted totally, partially or declined.
